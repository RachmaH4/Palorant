HOLLOW

THREE SITES TEAM FOCUSED

"The only map with three bomb sites. Requires strong team coordination and map control."

STRATEGIC MAP

TACTICAL BRIEFING

Hollow is the only map in Palorant featuring three bomb sites, demanding exceptional team coordination and strategic map control. The complex layout with multiple pathways and elevated positions creates dynamic engagements where information gathering and rotation timing are crucial.

KEY FEATURES

  • Three bomb sites (A, B, C)
  • Multiple rotation pathways
  • Team coordination essential
  • Complex map control

COMBAT STRATEGIES

  • ① Assign roles for each site control
  • ② Use recon abilities for information
  • ③ Control mid to enable fast rotations
  • ④ Fake attacks to confuse defenders
