Palorant Championship Series Announced

July 3, 2025 4 min read

Get ready for the ultimate competitive showdown! Palorant is proud to officially announce the inaugural Palorant Championship Series (PCS), a global esports tournament featuring the best teams from around the world. With a prize pool of $1,000,000 and international glory on the line, this is the event you don't want to miss.

Key Highlights

  • Global open qualifiers starting August 2025
  • $1,000,000 total prize pool
  • Grand Finals to be held in Tokyo, Japan
  • Special in-game event and exclusive rewards

Tournament Structure

The PCS will begin with regional open qualifiers across North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and South America. Top teams from each region will advance to the International Group Stage before competing in the Grand Finals event in Tokyo this December.

Schedule Overview

  • August 1-30: Regional Open Qualifiers
  • September 10-20: Group Stage
  • December 5-8: Grand Finals in Tokyo

Exclusive In-Game Event

Celebrate the Championship Series with a limited-time in-game event, featuring:

  • Special Championship-themed skins
  • Event Pass with free and premium rewards
  • Watch & Earn drops via official streams

"PCS is more than a tournament — it's a celebration of the Palorant community and competitive spirit. We can't wait to see new legends rise."

- Esports Director, Lina Tanaka

How to Register

Team Registration

  • Form a team of 5 players
  • Register via the official PCS website
  • Complete verification before July 25

Solo Players

  • Join community-run qualifier teams
  • Find teams via the official Palorant Discord
  • Compete in open qualifier brackets

Availability

The Championship Event Pass and in-game drops will be available starting August 1, 2025. Don't miss your chance to participate, whether on the battlefield or as a spectator!

For registration details and full tournament rules, visit our news page.
