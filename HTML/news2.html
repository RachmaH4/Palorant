Season 3 Battle Pass Details

June 27, 2025 3 min read

Palorant's latest Battle Pass for Season 3 is here, bringing an array of brand-new rewards, cosmetics, and content for players to unlock. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a fresh recruit, there's something for everyone in this update.

What's New in Season 3 Battle Pass?

  • 3 Exclusive Weapon Skin Bundles
  • New Player Cards & Sprays
  • Exclusive Melee Weapon Skin
  • Premium and Free Reward Tracks

Top Rewards Highlights

The Premium track offers some of the most sought-after cosmetics Palorant has ever released. Here's what players can look forward to:

  • Oblivion Phantom: Futuristic-themed skin with custom effects
  • Redstorm Knife: A deadly, sleek melee weapon
  • Champion's Edge Card: Show off your victories with pride
  • Exclusive Victory Animations for select weapons

How to Unlock Rewards

The Battle Pass includes both a free and premium track. Players can unlock free rewards by simply playing matches and completing daily and weekly missions. For those wanting the full experience, the Premium Pass can be purchased in the in-game store for 1,000 VP.

"Season 3's Battle Pass was designed to celebrate player creativity and offer unique rewards for every type of competitor."

- Battle Pass Lead, Kaito Ishikawa

Season Duration

Season 3 officially runs from June 27, 2025 to August 15, 2025. Make sure to claim all your rewards before the season ends!

Availability

The Battle Pass and its rewards are live now across all regions. Check out the full reward list in-game and start earning those cosmetics today.
