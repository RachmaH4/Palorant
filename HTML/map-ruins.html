RUINS

SNIPER'S PARADISE EXPERT

"A high-altitude map with open mid-control and rotating pathways"

LONG-RANGE MAP

TACTICAL BRIEFING

Ruins is a high-altitude battleground featuring expansive sightlines and open mid-control areas that favor long-range engagements. The map's rotating pathways create dynamic flanking opportunities, requiring teams to maintain constant map awareness and adapt their positioning. Snipers dominate the vertical gameplay while mobile agents can exploit the rotating routes for surprise attacks.

KEY FEATURES

  • High-altitude terrain
  • Rotating pathways
  • Long sightlines
  • Open mid-control

COMBAT STRATEGIES

  • ① Control high ground for vision advantage
  • ② Time rotations with pathway changes
  • ③ Use smokes to block sniper sightlines
  • ④ Coordinate flanks with pathway rotations
