AGENT DETAILS

Cobalt

DUELIST

"Strike from the shadows, leave no trace."

Cobalt is a high-mobility duelist specializing in rapid engagements and disengagements. His abilities allow him to reposition quickly during fights, making him unpredictable and deadly in one-on-one combat situations.

Q - SHADOW DIVE

Teleport a short distance in the direction you're moving. Can be used while airborne.

E - PHANTOM STRIKE

Empowers your next 2 shots to deal 50% more damage but costs 20 health per shot.

C - NIGHT SHROUD

Become invisible for 3 seconds when standing still. Moving or attacking breaks the effect.

X - SHADOW ASSAULT

Gain 3 charges of Shadow Dive and Phantom Strike for 10 seconds. Kills refresh all charges.
