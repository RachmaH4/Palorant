MIRAGE

VERTICAL COMBAT ADVANCED

"Verticality defines this map, with ropes and elevation changes creating dynamic engagements."

VERTICAL MAP

TACTICAL BRIEFING

Mirage is a map defined by its dramatic verticality, featuring multiple rope ascenders and elevation changes that create unique sightlines and engagement opportunities. The multi-level design rewards players who master movement mechanics and vertical positioning.

KEY FEATURES

  • Multiple elevation levels
  • Rope ascenders for vertical movement
  • Unique high-ground sightlines
  • Dynamic engagement angles

COMBAT STRATEGIES

  • ① Control high ground positions
  • ② Use ropes for surprise attacks
  • ③ Watch for enemies below/above
  • ④ Utilize vertical mobility agents
