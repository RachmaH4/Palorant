New Agent Revealed: Phantom

June 15, 2025 3 min read

Palorant is thrilled to introduce Phantom, the game's first stealth-based agent, arriving June 22 as part of our Season 3 update. This mysterious operative brings unprecedented deception tactics to the battlefield.

Key Features

  • First true stealth agent in Palorant
  • Designed for advanced mind games
  • Counter-balanced with audible cues
  • Part of Season 3's "Shadow Warfare" theme

Abilities Overview

Shroud Step (Q)

Briefly turn invisible (3s) and leave behind a decoy that mimics your movement pattern. Costs 200 credits.

Mirage (E)

Deploy a controllable phantom copy that can fake shooting. Lasts 8 seconds. 30s cooldown.

Shadow Swap (C)

Swap positions with any active decoy. Creates audible teleportation sound. 2 charges per round.

Vanishing Act (X)

Ultimate: Become completely invisible for 8 seconds. Firing breaks stealth. 7 ultimate points.

Developer Insights

"Phantom represents our most ambitious agent design yet. We wanted to create gameplay that rewards creativity and prediction rather than pure mechanical skill. His abilities will fundamentally change how players approach both attacking and defending." - Lead Agent Designer, Marco Rodriguez

Strategic Tips

Playing AS Phantom

  • Use decoys to fake site executes
  • Combine invisibility with off-angle positioning
  • Save Shadow Swap for emergency escapes

Playing AGAINST Phantom

  • Listen for distinctive audio cues
  • Watch for slight visual distortions when invisible
  • Control areas to limit his mobility options

Availability

Phantom will be unlockable through:

  • New Agent Contract (free track)
  • Immediate unlock in Premium Battle Pass
  • Standalone purchase for 1000 Valorant Points

Season 3 begins June 22 - check our news page for the full patch notes.
