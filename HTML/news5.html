Patch 3.2 Balance Changes

May 20, 2025 6 min read

Patch 3.2 is almost here, and with it comes a host of balance changes aimed at keeping Palorant's competitive integrity intact. From agent tweaks to weapon adjustments, here's everything you need to know before you queue up your next match.

Agent Balance Changes

  • Viper: Toxic Screen cooldown increased from 6s to 8s.
  • Reaper: Shadowstep audio cue made louder.
  • Sage: Slow Orb radius reduced by 10%.
  • Phantom: Mirage cooldown increased from 30s to 40s.

Weapon Adjustments

  • Guardian: Fire rate increased from 4.75 to 5.0.
  • Bulldog: ADS burst spread slightly reduced.
  • Marshal: Equip time decreased from 1.0s to 0.85s.

Competitive Matchmaking

Matchmaking now prioritizes party MMR average over highest-rated player. This ensures fairer lobbies and reduced match disparity.

Performance Improvements

Optimized map loading times by up to 15% and reduced in-game memory usage on lower-end devices.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed issue where Phantom's decoy would occasionally get stuck on ledges.
  • Resolved rare crash when switching weapons during teleport animation.
  • Corrected visual artifacts on Ascent's B-site doorway.

For a full list of changes, check our official patch notes on the Palorant News page.
