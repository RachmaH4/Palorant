ABYSS

CLOSE QUARTERS ADVANCED

"Dual teleporters connect sites, enabling rapid rotations"

CLOSE-COMBAT MAP

TACTICAL BRIEFING

Abyss is a close-quarters combat map dominated by two teleporter systems that enable lightning-fast rotations between bomb sites. The confined spaces and verticality create intense firefights where map control and teleporter timing are key to victory.

KEY FEATURES

  • Dual teleporter systems
  • Close-quarters combat focus
  • Multi-level verticality
  • Fast-paced rotations

COMBAT STRATEGIES

  • ① Control teleporter exits with traps
  • ② Use smokes to block vision in tight spaces
  • ③ Coordinate flashes with teammates
  • ④ Play retakes with utility advantage
