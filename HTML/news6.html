Community Skin Contest Winners

May 15, 2025 3 min read

Our very first Community Weapon Skin Contest has come to a close — and you absolutely delivered. After reviewing over a thousand incredible submissions from players around the world, we're thrilled to announce the winning designs making their way into Palorant soon!

Grand Prize Winner

"Neon Shatter Phantom" by LiraXArt dazzled both the judges and community vote with its vibrant colors and animated pulse effect. Expect to see it in the store this June!

Runners-Up

  • "Cyber Oni Guardian" by KazeDraws
  • "Pixel Pulse Classic" by BitsyByte
  • "Vortex Vandal" by NightTide

Each of these fantastic designs will be available as limited-time bundles throughout the upcoming season.

Special Thanks

We're overwhelmed by the creativity and talent of our community. Thank you to everyone who participated — and keep those designs coming, because we're planning another contest later this year!

See the full gallery of submissions and honorable mentions on our Palorant News page.
