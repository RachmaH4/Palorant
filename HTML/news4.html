New Map: Ascent Coming Next Week

May 28, 2025 2 min read

Prepare to elevate your strategies — Palorant is proud to announce Ascent, a brand new map arriving next week. Set in a futuristic Italian cityscape, Ascent features vertical combat spaces and open mid-control engagements that will challenge even the most seasoned players.

Map Highlights

  • Open central courtyard that dictates map control
  • Two bomb sites with multiple elevation points
  • Deployable doors to block site entrances
  • Futuristic cityscape visuals inspired by Venice

Gameplay Impact

The open mid section of Ascent is crucial for both attackers and defenders. Teams that can maintain mid control will have access to flexible rotations and creative flanking opportunities. Deployable doors on both sites add a fresh tactical layer, allowing players to temporarily block off key choke points or trap opponents inside a bombsite.

"Ascent was built to encourage vertical play and dynamic mid-control battles. We wanted to give players a map where every angle matters, and split-second decisions could turn the tide."

- Map Designer, Elena Russo

Availability

Ascent will be added to both Unrated and Competitive queues starting June 3, 2025. A dedicated Ascent-only queue will also be available for the first week so players can master its layout and vertical play options.
